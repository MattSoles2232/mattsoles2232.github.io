About

Our Story

Compound Bros. was founded by brothers Matt and Anthony Soles — two passionate food lovers with a mission to turn simple meals into unforgettable experiences. What began as weekend cooking experiments soon evolved into a shared obsession with flavor, texture, and the perfect finishing touch: compound butter.

We realized that something as simple as butter could be transformed into a powerhouse of flavor when infused with herbs, spices, and fresh ingredients — and so, the idea for Compound Bros. was born. From savory blends like Garlic & Herb or Smoked Chipotle to sweet favorites like Maple Cinnamon, every recipe is handcrafted in small batches using only real, high-quality ingredients.

Our commitment to excellence goes beyond what’s in the jar. As a family-run business, we take pride in every detail — from sourcing to packaging — ensuring that each butter not only tastes amazing, but tells a story of care, creativity, and culinary passion. Whether you're grilling, roasting, baking, or simply spreading, our butters are designed to enhance your meals and spark conversation at the table.

At Compound Bros., we don’t just make butter — we make food better.
